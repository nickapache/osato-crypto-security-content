🔬 Investigative Feature · DL News · Multi-source Expert Interviews

DeFi protocols continue to get hacked due to the same basic maths problem

This is the article that shows Osato does more than break news. He interviewed multiple smart contract security experts to investigate why the same class of vulnerability keeps getting exploited across the industry. The vulnerability is precision loss: Solidity uses only integers, so any division that produces a fraction gets rounded down. Attackers use flash loans to run that rounding error thousands of times in a single block, turning tiny fractions into large stolen sums. Osato spoke with security expert Webb, who called for a "cultural change" treating rounding errors with the same urgency as reentrancy attacks. Smart contract auditor Joe Dakwa told DL News that fuzz testing and invariant testing should become standard practice. The article documented a pattern of attacks across Raft Finance ($3.6M flash loan exploit), Midas Capital, Onyx Protocol, Compound Finance forks, and Hundred Finance, all hit by the same underlying maths flaw. This is exactly the kind of systemic security analysis Hacken publishes in its threat landscape reports.

DL News · May 2026

DxSale $7.3M BNB Chain drain — a 2023 Decurity warning that went unheeded for three years

Osato reported on the $7.3M drain of over 1,400 legacy liquidity locker positions on BNB Chain. The exploit used a hidden backdoor: contract ownership had been silently transferred 269 days before the attack, passing through 80 intermediate wallets to obscure the trail. The attacker reset the lock fee to 1 wei, backdated lock expiry timestamps, and drained pooled BNB in a single orchestrated transaction. Security firm Decurity had disclosed the core risk in DxSale's locking mechanism back in 2023. The vulnerability sat unpatched for years in contracts holding funds from over 1,400 liquidity providers. Possible insider involvement was flagged by on-chain researchers. This story demonstrates Osato's ability to cover legacy contract risks, access control failures, and the long tail of unpatched DeFi infrastructure.
